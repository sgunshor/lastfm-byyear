How to play

  • W/S: accelerate/brake. A/D: steer. Space: hold to drift for bonus points.
  • Stay on the asphalt ring to earn steady points. Off-track slows scoring and risks obstacles.
  • Hit obstacles and you'll lose speed and points. Drift cleanly to build combos.
  • Toggle Prototype vs Full mode: primitives only vs textured + loaded assets.
  • Mobile: use the on-screen buttons. Mouse/touch drag on the canvas pans the camera.
